# 📋 Rise of the Shadowbound - Development Roadmap

## Done
- [x] **Dark Fantasy Theme Overhaul**: Redesigned the layout, dashboard, trials, trivia, memory match, and settings pages to fully embrace the deep purple, obsidian, and glowing lavender aesthetic.
- [x] **Master RPG Interface**: Integrated the branching narrative, character profile, inventory, and spellbook into a gorgeous split-screen dashboard on the Story Page.
- [x] **Character Forging**: Fully functional character creator with class selection, attribute sliders, and dark fantasy traits.
- [x] **Arcane Trials**: Implemented Trivia Night and Memory Match mini-games with persistent scoring tied to the active character.
- [x] **Soul Dashboard**: Real-time tracking of time spent in the realm and high scores from the trials.

## In Progress
- [ ] **Dynamic Story Consequences**: Connect choices in the story to character attributes (e.g., requiring high Strength to break a door, or high Intelligence to read the tome).
- [ ] **Interactive Inventory & Spellbook**: Allow players to find items and learn spells during the story, which then appear in their companion panel.

## Future Ideas
- [ ] **Combat System**: A simple turn-based combat encounter with shadow beasts using the spellbook and weapons.
- [ ] **Sound Effects & Ambient Music**: Atmospheric background tracks and audio feedback for choices and spell casting.
